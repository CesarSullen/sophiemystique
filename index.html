Sophie Mystique

Bio

Houston based Cuban-American artist, Sophie Mystique fuses Rhythm & Blues with Pop and Bossa Nova as her signature style, displaying not only a versatile repertoire but also rich vocal technique, and a beautiful cultural diversity. The artist embodies a musical dream of sensuality and delicateness through the smooth yet powerful quality of her voice, delivering captivating performances that are geared to create an immersive experience and a genuine connection with the audience.
